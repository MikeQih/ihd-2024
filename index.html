Welcome to IHD 2024 Virtual Exhibition

Innovations in Healthcare Design (IHD) 2024 is an annual joint showcase of our students' healthcare efforts across the various pillars/clusters in the below Healthcare courses, namely:

  • 01.101 Technologies for Sustainable Global Health (SMT)
  • 02.230HT Health Communication and Behavior Change (HASS)
  • 30.123 Healthcare Product Design (EPD)

Over the course of a term, students have teamed up with practicing clinicians and professionals from various healthcare partners across Singapore to deliver innovative healthcare design solutions to current unmet clinical needs.

Join us (virtually) to witness their design ideas and discover the design process that ultimately resulted in the realization of these innovative design solutions.
