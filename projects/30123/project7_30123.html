SterilEase is an automatic hand sanitizer dispensing device designed primarily as a modular mount to be installed around any door or entrance in a hospital. Its working principle is the simultaneous sanitization of a healthcare worker’s hands as they are entering a ward. This newly developed product is also designed to meet the specific needs of hospitals by providing a non-contact sanitation solution.
